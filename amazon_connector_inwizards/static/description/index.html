Amazon Odoo Connector

Enhance your Amazon business and streamline operations by integrating Amazon with Odoo for improved back-office efficiency.

As Amazon continues to expand globally, your business can seize the chance to grow on an international scale. However, this growth brings the challenge of effectively managing it. Highlights

Order Processing & Fulfillment Made Easy

Consolidate all your Amazon orders—both Fulfilled by Merchant (FBM) and Fulfilled by Amazon (FBA)—from various marketplaces into Odoo. Enjoy a seamless order processing experience and send shipment tracking details directly to your customers, enhancing their satisfaction with every purchase.

Effortless Stock Synchronization

Eliminate the anxiety of overselling or underselling. With automatic stock level synchronization between Amazon and Odoo, you can ensure that all your marketplaces reflect accurate, up-to-date inventory, safeguarding your sales potential.

Simplified Catalog Management

Efficiently manage your product listings by synchronizing product details and variations between Odoo and Amazon. Use CSV and Excel files to adjust pricing and easily map your Amazon products within Odoo for a hassle-free experience.

Streamlined Settlement Report Processing

Consolidate all your Amazon expenses, commissions, and payments by importing Settlement Reports into Odoo. Process and reconcile these reports seamlessly, ensuring your accounting is always in check.

Enhanced Order Cancellation Tracking

Elevate your customer service by keeping a precise record of order cancellations. Fetch cancellation data from Amazon to Odoo, and perform cancellations directly from Odoo to maintain accuracy and responsiveness.

Efficient Invoicing & Refund Management

Wave goodbye to accounting headaches! Manage invoicing for your Amazon orders entirely within Odoo, and upload invoices directly to Amazon. Keep track of refund requests and process them smoothly from one central location.

Hassle-Free Inbound Shipments

Manage inbound shipments effortlessly through Odoo! Create shipment plans, add products, and send them to Amazon with ease. If you prefer, generate shipments in Amazon and retrieve them in Odoo using the Shipment ID.

Automated FBA Stock Adjustments

Stay on top of your FBA inventory with Amazon’s Inventory Adjustment Reports. Automatically import these reports into Odoo to make necessary stock adjustments, ensuring your warehouse data remains accurate.

Seamless Removal Order Processing

Need to remove inventory from FBA warehouses? Create removal orders in either Odoo or Amazon, specify your preferences for returns or disposals, and process everything smoothly in Odoo.

Multi-Channel Fulfillment Support

Managing multiple sales channels? Utilize Amazon's Multi-Channel Fulfillment (MCF) services by preparing and submitting outbound order information directly from Odoo to Amazon’s fulfillment centers.

Robust Automation Features

Reduce the burden of routine tasks by enabling automation for order imports, stock synchronization, price updates, and more. Let Odoo handle the repetitive actions while you focus on scaling your business.

Connect Multiple Seller Accounts

Expand your global reach effortlessly by connecting multiple seller accounts to your Odoo instance, including various marketplaces for each account.

Comprehensive Seller Rating Insights

Gain valuable insights into seller performance by importing feedback reports into Odoo. Review ratings by order, customer, and more, enabling you to take targeted corrective actions as needed.

VAT Calculation Made Simple

Participate in Amazon’s VAT calculation service seamlessly. Our connector allows you to store Amazon invoice numbers for posted and paid invoices directly in Odoo, simplifying your tax processes.

Tailored Amazon Fulfillment Configurations

With various fulfillment programs like PAN EU, CFN, EFN, and MCI, our integration ensures that necessary configurations—like warehouse setups and tax adjustments—are automatically handled in Odoo, so you can operate without a hitch.

Features

Order Management

  • Import Orders from Amazon to Odoo

    (FBM: Shipped & Unshipped, FBA: Shipped & Pending)

  • Export Shipping Details with Package Tracking Number from Odoo to Amazon
  • View FBM & FBA orders separately in Odoo
  • Check FBA Order Cancellations on Amazon and update the order status in Odoo

Returns & Refund

  • Manage FBA Returns by importing return reports, validating incoming shipment and creating credit notes.

Settlement Report

  • Download, Process & Reconcile Amazon Settlement Report in Odoo
  • Get Amazon fees, Commissions & other charges from Amazon Settlement Report and post it to Odoo Accounting
  • Reconcile Orders, Invoices & Credit Notes against the settlement report
  • Generate reimbursement invoices if Amazon provides you reimbursement in any case

FBA Inventory

Inbound Shipment

  • Import Amazon Inbound Shipment In Odoo
  • Send Inbound Shipments from Odoo to Amazon; Create a shipment plan, add products to it with the appropriate quantity and submit the shipment plan to Amazon
  • Update shipment information for Inbound Shipment and Create and Print Package/Pellet labels/Unique Labels.
  • Manage Amazon Partnered / Non-partnered Carriers for Inbound Shipments to Amazon
  • Track status of Inbound Shipments
  • Track discrepancies in Quantity/Products that are sent & received

Removal Order

  • Create Removal Order in Odoo for sellable or unsellable inventory and send it to Amazon
  • Import removal orders to Odoo which are processed by Amazon when the seller has configured Auto Removal on their Seller Central account; Process the removal orders in Odoo after importing them

Inventory Adjustments

  • Import Inventory Adjustment report from Amazon to Odoo and accordingly carry out the stock adjustment in your Odoo warehouse

FBA Shipment

  • Create Orders, Invoices & Process deliveries in Odoo by importing & processing FBA Shipment Report from Amazon

Products

  • Sync Products between Amazon & Odoo
  • Export Products (Using CSV and Excel)
  • Update Product Information
  • Export/Update Product Price, & Stock Levels

Other Features
